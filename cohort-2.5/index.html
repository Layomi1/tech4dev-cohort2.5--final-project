The participation of parents in the educational process means that teachers and parents share the responsibility to teach students and work together to achieve educational goals. To this end, the organization mandates that teachers invite parents to regular academic meetings and events and that parents voluntarily commit to prioritizing these goals.
It helps when the parents feel happier and more involved with their children’s education. A good connection with the school lets parents understand the curriculum better and the advancement of their children. It also helps them feel more comfortable and happier with the quality of education. It can even motivate those who did not finish their education to continue it.
Parental involvement also improves teacher performance. When parents have better communication with teachers, they learn to value the work and the challenges that teachers face, which makes our teachers feel appreciated.
