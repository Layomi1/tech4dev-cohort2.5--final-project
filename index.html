The participation of parents in the educational process will enable teachers and parents to share the responsibility to teach students and work together to achieve educational goals.
A good comprehension of the website helps the parent understand the curriculum better and the progress of their children. It also helps them feel more comfortable and happier with the quality of education they're receiving. It can even motivate those who did not finish their education to continue it.
Parental involvement also improves teacher performance. When parents have better communication with teachers, they learn to value the work and the challenges that teachers face, which makes our teachers feel appreciated.

About Us

Bridging your transitional journey

U-learn is a website catering for senior high school students to enable them transition to tertiary levels. Students can gain better understanding of their current subjects being taught, learn new ones, as well as gain technical and vocational skills, for employment, decent jobs and entrepreneurship.
Every one of our online courses have been carefully designed with online learning in mind, focusing on the areas of study and skillsets that are most in-demand in the current job market.
